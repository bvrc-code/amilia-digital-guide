About Blue Valley Rec

Our Vision: Enriching Lives, Strengthening a Spirit of Community

Our Mission: We will champion community and personal growth through dynamic, lifelong recreation experiences.

Our Core Values: Integrity · Communication · Customer Service · Teamwork

Service Area: Formed in August 1986 by a concerned citizen group to address the shortage of recreation opportunities in the area, BVRC was formed under the auspices of the Blue Valley School District and its patrons are the primary focus of our recreation programming.

Scholarships

Residents of the Blue Valley School District meeting certain guidelines may be eligible for scholarship assistance for their children ages 18 and younger. If approved, scholarship funds cover 50% of the program fee and applicants are asked to pay the remaining balance. For more information, visit the Scholarship section of our website.

Registration Policy

To attend the activities, classes, and events listed in this guide, you must first register before attending. An account must be set up prior to registering for those being registered for the activity.

  • Registration: Fees are due at the time of registration. We reserve the right to cancel, combine, or divide classes based on enrollment. Information is subject to change regarding dates, times, locations, and instructors. Please check our website for updated information.
  • Resident/Non-Resident Participation: Residents living within BVSD boundaries or owning property within the BVSD boundaries receive a discount on many classes and activities.
  • Wait List: If a class has met its maximum enrollment, patrons have the option to add a name to the wait list for up to three classes per program session. Should a spot open, staff will contact those on the wait list to fill the available openings. If enrollment from a wait list is accepted, that name will be removed from all other wait lists for that program session. Sorry, patrons cannot be enrolled in a program and be on a wait list for another session of the same program.
  • Class Attendance: Sorry, children are not allowed to attend classes with their parents. Siblings of participants are not permitted in teaching areas.

Refunds

A full refund will be granted to those who cancel or withdraw from most programs four (4) business days prior to the start date ($10 fee when cancelling less than four (4) business days, defined as Monday–Friday), with exceptions listed below. A pro-rated refund may be granted (less the $10 fee) if no more than half of the program dates have been held. No refund will be issued when more than half of the program dates have been held or the program has ended.

  • Gymnastics: Registrations will not be available or prorated after the second class, including refunds or cancellations. A full refund will be granted to those who cancel or withdraw from a class four (4) business days prior to the start date without a cancellation fee. You may receive a prorated refund if you cancel before the start of the second class period, but you will incur a $10 processing fee along with your refund. No refunds, make-up classes, or Open Gymnastics passes will be given for any missed attendance (including cancellations after the 2nd class).
  • Dance: Registrations may receive a prorated refund if you cancel before the start of the second class, but you will acquire a $10 processing fee along with your refund. No refunds or make-up classes will be given for any missed attendance (including cancellations after the second class).
  • Aquatics: Registrations will be refunded 100% when cancelled 10 days prior to start of the activity. A $10 processing fee will be assessed to cancellations made from 9–5 days of the start of the activity. No refunds will be issued for cancellations within 4 or fewer days of the start of the activity.
